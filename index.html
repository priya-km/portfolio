ABOUT ME





Hello world!

Hello! I'm Priya K Maharban, a passionate creator at the intersection of web development and UI/UX design. My technological journey began in childhood, self-learning Adobe programs, which sparked a lifelong interest in digital creation. College years further fueled my enthusiasm as I explored coding and discovered a deep affinity for both design and development.
Today, I combine my artistic flair with technical skills, specializing in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, Django, React, React Native, alongside a keen eye for UI/UX design principles, graphic design, and video editing. I'm on the lookout for full-time roles where I can bring my creative and coding talents to the forefront, particularily within a web developer or UI/UX designer role and ideally in settings that allow for remote work or in-person/hybrid in the New York City area.
My aim is to be part of a team that pushes boundaries in digital solutions, contributing to projects that resonate with users on a functional and aesthetic level. If my blend of creative vision and development expertise excites you, I'd love to connect! For contact details, please visit the Contact section of this site. I'm eager to explore how we can create impactful digital experiences together.
